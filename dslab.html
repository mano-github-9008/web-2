WELCOME TO DATA SCIENCE LAB

Initial Planning

The plan to create a Data Science Lab was initiated in late 2022 by the HOD of Data Science, Dr. Abuzar Ansari, with a vision to foster innovation.

Construction Phase

After months of planning, construction of the lab began with state-of-the-art infrastructure in the month of september 2023.

Equipment Installation

High-end computing machines and AI research tools were installed to support innovation.

Lab Inauguration

The lab was officially inaugurated and opened for students, researchers, and professionals in the month of December 2023.

Successful Operations

Today, the Data Science Lab is a hub of groundbreaking research and innovation.
