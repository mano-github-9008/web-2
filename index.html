Our Facilities

Data Science Lab

Our cutting-edge Data Science Lab is equipped with high-performance computing systems, providing the perfect environment for AI and machine learning research.

Solar Panel Facility

We have a state-of-the-art solar panel installation that powers our servers and computing systems, ensuring sustainability and reducing carbon footprint.

Server Room

Our secure and highly efficient server room houses the latest hardware, ensuring fast data processing and secure storage for all our data science projects.

Collaboration Room

Our collaboration room provides a space for team discussions, brainstorming, and collaborative work, equipped with the latest communication tools.

Research Library

The library is stocked with the latest books, journals, and resources on data science, machine learning, and AI to keep our team up-to-date with the latest trends.

AI Workshop

Our AI workshop is a creative space for hands-on experimentation and prototyping, where ideas come to life through coding and testing of AI models.

Department Vision

To evolve as a centre of proficiency in Data Analytics, and develop high-quality professionals and researchers who will solve grand challenges and innovate through world-class research in the field of Data Science.

Department Mission

Our Mission is to provide highest quality of teaching, learning opportunities with well researched and carefully crafted curriculum. We integrate curriculum into practical, relevant solutions to address business and societal challenges. To empower students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the rapidly evolving field of data science. We provide a comprehensive and interdisciplinary education that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and ethical data practices. Pushing the boundaries of data science through cutting-edge research that addresses real-world challenges. We strive to advance in this field, contribute to knowledge creation, and drive innovations that benefit the society.
