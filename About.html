Our Mission

  • Our Mission is to provide highest quality of teaching, learning opportunities with well researched and carefully crafted curriculum.
  • We integrate curriculum into practical, relevant solutions to address business and societal challenges.
  • To empower students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the rapidly evolving field of data science. We provide a comprehensive and interdisciplinary education that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving, and ethical data practices.
  • Pushing the boundaries of data science through cutting-edge research that addresses real-world challenges. We strive to advance in this field, contribute to knowledge creation, and drive innovations that benefit the society.

    • Department Vision

    To evolve as a centre of proficiency in Data Analytics, and develop high-quality professionals and researchers who will solve grand challenges and innovate through world-class research in the field of Data Science.
